Terrasphere Build Editor

Toggle Instructions


  1. Select your masteries and armor weight.
    • You must select at least 3 masteries and no more than 5
    • You can only have 1 Alter Mastery at maximum
    • Certain masteries have compatability restrictions; check the wiki for more info
  2. Select the ranks for your Equipment and Masteries.
  3. Select the actions you wish to take.
    • If you have selected light armor, you may choose 5 additional actions.
    • Otherwise you may select 4 additional actions.
  4. Your final build will be displayed.
    • Click on the mastery icon in each action card to change the roll code according to the selected mastery.
    • Numbers in the STATS section already take your actions and equipment ranks into account.
